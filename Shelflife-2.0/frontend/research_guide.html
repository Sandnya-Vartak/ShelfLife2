Smart Storage

Smart Storage Guide

Notes gathered from real kitchens and published pantry science to help you keep food at its best.

Why it matters

Keeping track of expiry dates and acting before things degrade protects your budget and pulls emissions down. Use ShelfLife to schedule meals around what is nearing its best use window.

Research-backed tips

  • Wrap leafy greens in a paper towel and slip them into a breathable bag to extend crispness.
  • Portion bulk buys right away, freeze the extras, and note each bag in ShelfLife so nothing hides in the freezer.
  • Compare the consumption summary to your shopping runs to spot ingredients that vanish quickly or linger too long.

Every alert you respect instead of dismissing keeps your income and sustainability story aligned.
